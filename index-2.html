About Us

Cooking the Flavors of Home Since Day One

Brighto Restaurant started in the heart of Oyo State with one goal: to serve Nigerian food that tastes like it was made by family. Every pot of stew, every mound of pounded yam, and every skewer of suya is prepared fresh, daily, using recipes passed down through generations.

Today we feed families, students, office workers, travellers passing through, and organizers hosting unforgettable events — all without losing the care that started it all.

  • Fresh Daily — nothing sits overnight, every dish is cooked to order or same-day.
  • Local Ingredients — sourced from trusted Oyo State markets and farms.
  • Fast Delivery — hot food, on time, to your home or office.
  • Trusted for Events — from birthdays to weddings, we cater with consistency.
